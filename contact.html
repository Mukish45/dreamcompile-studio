Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of projects do you work on?

I specialize in AI and machine learning projects, particularly involving large language models, chatbots, data analysis, and full-stack applications. I'm always interested in innovative projects that push the boundaries of technology.

Are you available for freelance work?

Yes, I'm open to freelance and consulting opportunities! I work with startups, established companies, and individual entrepreneurs on AI implementation, data science projects, and technical consulting.

Do you offer mentoring or coaching?

I occasionally take on mentoring opportunities for aspiring data scientists and AI developers. If you're looking for guidance in your AI journey, feel free to reach out and we can discuss how I might be able to help.

Can you speak at events or conferences?

Absolutely! I enjoy sharing knowledge about AI, machine learning, and data science. I've spoken at various tech meetups and conferences. Contact me with details about your event and I'll be happy to consider it.

What's your preferred communication method?

For initial contact, email works best as it allows me to give thoughtful responses. For ongoing projects, I'm flexible and can adapt to whatever communication style works best for the team (Slack, Discord, video calls, etc.).

Yes, I work with clients globally! I'm comfortable with remote collaboration and have experience working across different time zones. I can adapt my schedule to accommodate important meetings and project requirements.
